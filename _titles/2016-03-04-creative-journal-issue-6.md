## from Paul David Adkin's
### La Llorona, Described by Herself

Not a guera,
guajira,
not a girl
to bring home to your mother.

I don't have legs of a Bunny
or toes of a goat.

My teeth aren't
perfectly straight.

No ring,
or beads encircling my neck.

I'd ask my children.

Go talk to my chulo.

El diablo can tell you.

Call for me
in front
of a mirror.
